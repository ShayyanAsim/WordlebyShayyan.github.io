Wordle

    How to Play

  • Start typing.The letters will appear in the boxes
  • Remove letters with backspace
  • Hit Enter/Return to submit the answer
  • Letters with green background are in the right spot
  • Letter with yellow background exist in the world, but are in the wrong spot.
  • Letters with gray background do not exist in the word
  • If you need a hint, click the ? icon. It will show at the bottom of the page.
  • If you press enter unnecessarily before completing the word you will lose a chace.
  • To close this press the i button again.
